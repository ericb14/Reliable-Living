Helping Restore Independence
with Passionate In-Home Care

We understand how difficult it can be to care for loved ones and friends who are no longer able to fully take care of themselves. The difficulty of entrusting strangers for assistance when the burden has become too great can be an emotionally exhausting task. That is why we at Reliable Living are here to make this process as easy and effortless as possible.

Our goal at Reliable Living is to provide our clients assistance that allows you or your loved you to refrain from entering an assisted living community, nursing home, or any other type of care facility. We know that it is more comfortable and convenient for you or your loved one to stay at home where there are less changes to the activities of daily life.

That is where Reliable comes into play. Reliable Living offers in-home care services for individuals and families in many different ways and for many different needs. We believe in providing you and your loved ones with compassion and the very best care available. Our services are conducted by highly trained and professional caregivers who are committed to providing the highest standards of home care services possible. We customize our care plans so they meet the special needs of our clients and are designed to work with what's best for you.

ABOUT US

We are a team. Our team consists not only of our employees, but every person who plays a part in successfully fulfilling the needs of our clients. We have found that every client is unique and requires a team focus of all participants. Our clients, their family, and our care team must all work together to make sure every need is met. We firmly believe this is best accomplished when everyone views our relationship as a team effort.

How do I get started?

Contact us by either phone or email. Then we will arrange a time to schedule a free no obligation assessment that will allow us to get to the core of you or your loved ones needs to make sure those needs are met

How quickly can services be provided?

Services typically start within 24-48 hours of the completed personal assessment and service contract agreement.

Where can services be provided?

Services can be provided wherever our clients reside.

How are caregivers selected?

All our caregivers go through an in-depth screening process when being hired. Then, we match you with caregivers we feel would be a great fit for you and your needs.

What happens if my caregiver is unavailable for a scheduled visit?

We have a team of certified caregivers to fill in if a staff member is unable to make a schedule visit due to unforeseen circumstances.

Who pays the caregiver you or me?

Reliable Living handles all the payroll regarding your caregivers.

Why should I use Reliable Living rather than hire someone myself?

At Reliable Living we strive to be the best. We hold ourselves to the highest possible standards so that we can give you or your loved ones the best care available.
